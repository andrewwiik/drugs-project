Our goal is to offer an easy way for people to turn in excess prescription drugs. We offer a variety of gift cards for specific prescription drugs that are known to be abused or to be gateways to more dangerous substances. The amount of the gift-card is based on the quantity of prescriptions turned over.

Which Prescriptions are Eligible for Gift Cards?

Codeine, Fentanyl, Amphetamine, Hydrocodone, Dihydrocodeinone, Oxycodone, Methylphenidate, and Morphine.

Which Brand Gift Cards are Offered?

We offer gift-cards for Target, BestBuy, Walmart, CVS, Walgreens and ShopRite.

Your Questions

The what, when, where, why, and how. The questions you are probably asking yourself right now.

What?

We buy-back your excess prescription drugs with gift-cards in order to prevent them from being abused or becoming a gateway for more dangerous substance abuse. We offer gift-cards in return for the prescriptions if they're eligible.

Where?

We offer a variety of drop-off locations to our users. Usually drop-off locations are your local police-station or pharmacy.

When?

Most of our drop-off locations are open 6 or 7 days a week (Mon - Sat) from 9am - 5pm, excluding holidays.

Why?

This program is offered because often when a doctor prescribes medication there is usually extra left-over due to various reasons like the patient forgetting to take them a few times or the patient feeling they do not need to take the drugs anymore.

How?

We buy-back the prescriptions through a process that starts on this website and ends at a physical drop-off location. You can find more about the specific steps below under "The Process".

The Process

Identity

First we need to confirm that you are who you say you are, this can be through uploading a picture of a valid form of ID. Valid forms of ID that are accepted include your passport or driver's license.

Eligibility

We need you to tell us to whom the prescription(s) you wish to turn belong. If the prescription(s) do not belong to you or a individual to whom you are the legal guardian of we cannot offer you gift-cards for them. We will still however take them free of charge to make sure they are disposed of properly.

Quantity

We need you to tell us exactly what type of drugs you are turning over and the exact amount of each.

Payment

Now that we know who you are, who the drugs belong to, and how much of each drug you are turning over we can now tell you how much in gift-cards we are willing to offer for them.

Decision

Now that you know the eligibility of all your drugs and how much we are willing to offer for them you can now decide whether you wish to continue the process. If you choose to continue the process this does not mean you obligated to continue through.

When and Where

If you choose to accept our offer you can now choose when and where you would like to complete the transaction. We will automatically set up an appointment at the drop-off location and give you all the necessary information to ensure the rest of the process goes smoothly.

This information includes a QR-Code that is tied to all the information you provided in the process, a note to carry with you en-route to the drop-off location in case you are stopped by the police. We also give you a print-out that includes all the information you provided in the process for your own records.
